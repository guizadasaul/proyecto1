About Us

Founded in 1899, FC Barcelona is more than just a football club it's a symbol of passion, tradition, and excellence. With a rich history and an unwavering commitment to our values, we have built a legacy that transcends the game. Our mission is to inspire, unite, and make a positive impact both on and off the field.

Join us on our journey as we continue to reach new heights, driven by the support of millions of fans worldwide. Together, we are Barça. Together, we are more than a club.
