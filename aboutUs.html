WHO ARE WE?

About Us

Hello, we are FC Barcelona, a club built on the foundation of passion, unity, and respect. Our mission goes beyond winning titles its about inspiring our fans, promoting positive change, and standing as a beacon of excellence in the world of sports. Since 1899, we have strived to create a legacy that transcends the game. Through our foundation and social programs, we work to empower young people, foster inclusion, and use the power of sport to make a difference. Every match, every achievement, and every act reflects our commitment to these values. We invite you to join us on this journey. Together, we can continue to make an impact, celebrating our rich history and paving the way for a brighter future. At Barça, we are more than a club we are a family, united by a shared mission.
